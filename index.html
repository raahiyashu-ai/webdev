Technology has dramatically transformed modern education. Online learning platforms allow students from different parts of the world to access high-quality courses and resources that were previously unavailable. Interactive tools, such as virtual simulations and educational videos, enhance understanding and engagement, making complex concepts easier to grasp. Moreover, technology enables personalized learning, allowing students to progress at their own pace based on their strengths and weaknesses. As a result, education has become more flexible, accessible, and tailored to individual needs, preparing learners to thrive in a fast-changing world.
